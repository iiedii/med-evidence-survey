Before start, please get to know your job:

For each of the following page, some pieces of evidence will be presented to you. They all are from one single video. You need to make a judgement at your shortest convenient time, predicting whether the video is relevant to the event. You need to choose one of the three answers:

  • Yes: Inferring from the evidence, you are certain that the video is relevant.
  • No: Inferring from the evidence, you are certain that the video is irrelevant.
  • Not sure: You can not conclude from the evidence.

Be aware that the evidence is generated by different methods. You don't need to know from which method it is generated. The presentation of the evidence can be in either images or snippets. For the snippet type of evidence, please mouseover the snippet to play it. Audio is also available when you play a snippet. Be prepared.

Note that your response time is also recorded. Please do this study EXCLUSIVELY. It is recommended you take a rest only once you finish a whole event. Log out manually. DO NOT hold on the question page if you were to have a break or were interrupted. Log out and log back in in this case to proceed the study.
